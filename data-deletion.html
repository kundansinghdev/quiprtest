User Data Deletion – Quipr

Quipr respects your privacy and your right to delete your data.

How to request data deletion

If you have used Quipr with Instagram or Facebook and want your data deleted:

  1. Send an email to manavhustles@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line: “Quipr Data Deletion Request”
  3. Include the email address associated with your account

We will verify your identity and delete all associated data within 30 days.

Data that will be deleted

  • User profile information
  • Connected Instagram account data
  • Automation rules, leads, and message logs

If you have questions, contact us at manavhustles@gmail.com.
